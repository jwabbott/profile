Joshua W. Abbott, JD - Short Bio

Josh Abbott serves as Director of J.D. Student Advising at Arizona State University's Sandra Day O'Connor College of Law 
and is a PhD candidate in Complex Adaptive Systems Science at ASU's College of Global Futures. He earned his bachelor 
and law degrees from Brigham Young University and practiced law at a private firm in Washington, DC, before coming to ASU
Law to serve as Executive Director of ASU's Center for Law, Science and Innovation.
